What makes Karma Manager special?

More than a simple anagram solver

Multi-Word Anagrams

Most anagram tools only find single-word rearrangements. Karma Manager finds multi-word combinations, turning "University of California, Davis" into "I saved oily victorian ruffians."

Huge Dictionaries

Search across nearly 80,000 words with dictionaries for US, UK, Canadian, and Australian English. Optionally include proper names, place names, and your own custom words.

Interesting Words Analysis

Not sure where to start? The "Interesting words" feature analyzes results to show which uncommon words appear most often, helping you find the most creative anagrams.

Include & Exclude

Steer your search by requiring specific words to appear in results, or excluding words you don't want. Narrow thousands of results down to the gems.

Animated Transformations

Watch letters glide from the original phrase into the anagram, one character at a time. Customize colors and timing. Export as GIF or video to share anywhere.

Favorites & Sync

Save your best finds, organize them by input phrase, and edit the word order with drag-and-drop tiles. Sync favorites across all your devices with a free account.

Share Links

Generate a shareable link for any favorite anagram. Recipients can view it on the web or import it directly into their own Karma Manager app.

Runs Everywhere

Available on iPhone, iPad, Android, macOS, and in any modern web browser. Your anagram toolkit is always within reach.
